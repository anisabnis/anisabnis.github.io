Anirudh Sabnis

I am an Architect at Akamai Technologies, where I build cost-performance models for Akamai's edge caches and develop systems for traffic allocation across edge clusters.

I completed my Ph.D. from the College of Information and Computer Sciences at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, advised by Prof. Ramesh Sitaraman, and was a member of the LIDS Lab.

Prior to that, I earned my undergraduate degree from NITK Surathkal in 2012 and worked at Akamai as a Software Engineer from 2012 to 2015.
